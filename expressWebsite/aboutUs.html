Concept Makers
Small dairy owners are not able to expand their business. Shopkeepers faces difficulties in managing the daily accounts, bills, customers details and records. Customers daily needs to go to the shop and purchase the milk. Customers are not able to communicate with shopkeepers easily if they have any query. Currently there is no existing system which solve problem of shopkeeper. Our system MilkPrism eases the work of shopkeepers by converting their offline process of making bills and daily entries of customers to online process. At the end of every month our system will generate the bills of the customers and customers will get it through emails. Customers can easily communicate with shopkeepers if they have any query or wants to edit their upcoming orders. Shopkeeper can post if they require any delivery boy. Any delivery boy can easily find job if any shopkeeper requires a delivery boy. Ishan Jagani

Introduction
Now-a-days small dairy owners are not able to expand their business because of tremendous competition in the market. Through our website they can easily expand their business. Our system will help shopkeepers by converting their offline process of making bills and daily entries to online process. It is also difficult for shopkeepers to manage the accounts and records of all the customer. Customers faces difficulties in finding the perfect shop. Customers will no longer need to go to the shop daily to buy milk, they get delivery by delivery boy which is hired by a Shopkeeper. Shopkeeper will not need to give monthly bills to the customers, customers will get it through email. Shopkeeper will no longer need to worry about hiring the delivery boy they can easily post on our website for the requirement of the delivery boy. If a delivery boy needs the job they can easily find the job through our website if any shopkeeper needs.
