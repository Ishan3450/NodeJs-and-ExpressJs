On Going Project

  • Small dairy owners are not able to expand their business. Shopkeepers faces difficulties in managing the daily accounts, bills, customers details and records. Customers daily needs to go to the shop and purchase the milk. Customers are not able to communicate with shopkeepers easily if they have any query. Currently there is no existing system which solve problem of shopkeeper. Our system MilkPrism eases the work of shopkeepers by converting their offline process of making bills and daily entries of customers to online process. At the end of every month our system will generate the bills of the customers and customers will get it through emails. Customers can easily communicate with shopkeepers if they have any query or wants to edit their upcoming orders. Shopkeeper can post if they require any delivery boy.
